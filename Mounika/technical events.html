TECHNICAL EVENTS

TECHNICAL PAPER PRESENTATION

OBJECTIVE :
The main objective of the competition that the participants has to keep in mind while preparing is to develope and present a holistic style of aproach which is able to motivate his/her audience.

The participant selects a topic from the given list collects information and presents a content filled and a desired information.
JUDGING CRITERIA :
Collection of basic data and correlation of data collected from different sources.

Clarity of artwork (slides,charts,graphs) Originality and Creativity of presenter in presenting, enhances his/her individual scores.

Proper organisation and delivery of the logical ideas and content that is entirely based on the selected topic.

Visual aids and technical aspects also enhances their scores, as it must be well constructed, easy to interpret amd must be used effectively.

Good communication skills and stage presence also is mandatory and will also be taken under consideration.

The judging criteria looks for the ability to stimulate and answer interesting questions, not only to clarify the technical aspectsof the work.
EVALUATION SCHEME :
  1. Originality and Creativity 20%
  2. Organisation 20%
  3. Clarity and Delivery 20%
  4. Knowledge of material 20%
  5. Over all presentation 20%


PROJECT EXPO

OBJECTIVE :
To develop professional good skills, self-learning ability and confidence to support and contribute to the growth of relevant industries.

This provides an opportunity for students to demostarte their learning experience.

This provides an opportunity for students to expose their ideas.

RULES :
A team can consist maximum of (4) "FOUR" members.
Students from different departments can be on the same team.
The teams will get a maximum of 10 minutes for the explanation.
"ENGLISH" is not mandatory for the explanation.
Participants can use "Ppt,Images,Printouts,Videos" for the explanation.
Participants can also use "3D Printed Parts" for their model.
The project should be a "WORKING MODEL".
SELECTION CRITERIA :
Clean,Neat and Crisp explanation about the project.
Neat project display.
Usage of "Proper images,Ciecuit diagrams, explaning the basic principle behind the working and it's an application" will help to improve your scores.
"Flawless Answers" for the questions asked by the judge.
